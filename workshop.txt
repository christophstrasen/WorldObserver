description=WorldObserver is a cooperative world-sensing engine for mid-level Lua mod developers.
description=Instead of hand-rolling OnTick scan batches and custom caches, you declare interest
description=in facts (scope, radius, staleness) and subscribe to ready-made observation streams.
description=
description=Good for mods that need rich, asynchronous information like:
description=- corpse squares near the player
description=- kitchens with specific zombie types
description=- vehicles under attack
description=
description=This is a developer library, not gameplay content. It does nothing on its own.
description=
description=GitHub (docs, source, examples):
description=https://github.com/christophstrasen/WorldObserver
description=
description=Dependencies (required by mod.info):
description=- StarlitLibrary
description=- DoggyLibrary
description=- reactivex
description=- LQR
description=- DREAMBase
